The coursework was spilt up into two main parts. Part A consisted of standard features and Part B consisted of advanced features.


The above screenshot demonstrates many of the standard features implemented in the coursework. The scene consists of a landscape formed of a several textures, and is lit by several light sources, each providing ambient, diffuse and specular components. The screenshot also shows the use of simple shadow mapping, creating shadows from various meshes on the terrain, and uses environment mapping to create a skybox around the scene.
